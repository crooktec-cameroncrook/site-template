Tucked away in Western Wyoming, Lincoln County is a land full of nature, history, and character. No matter where you are from, visiting Lincoln County will always feel like coming home. In it's classic western communities you will find American main streets lined with unique shopping, secret dining gems, and friendly smiles. Between those communities Rocky Mountain treasures lie in plain sight with world-class fishing, breathtaking scenery, and sunsets that embody the spirit of the West. Be warned, once you visit, you'll never want to leave.
